View the fleet dashboard:
  kubectl -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} port-forward svc/{{ include "poddoctor-hub.fullname" . }} 8090:{{ .Values.service.port }}
  open http://localhost:8090

Point a cluster's PodDoctor at this hub (values.yaml on the poddoctor chart):
  notifications:
    routes:
      - namespaces: ["*"]
        webhookURL: "http://{{ include "poddoctor-hub.fullname" . }}.{{ .Release.Namespace }}.svc:{{ .Values.service.port }}/ingest"
        webhookFormat: generic
        webhookToken: "<same token as auth.token above>"
